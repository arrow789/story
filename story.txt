A man walked down a road and discovered a coin on the ground. The coin had a detailed inscription on it that the man could not read. He picked it up and turned it over in his hand a couple times. One side was extremly smooth to the touch and reflected brightly. Then man took the coin back to his house to study it more closely.

The man cannot find any information about the coin on the Internet. "The coin must be special," he thought. He wanted to take out the coin to have a detailed look. However, the coin was disappeared from his pocket. The man search the whole house and couldn't find the coin. The second day, he passed through the same road and noticed, the coin was still at its original place!
